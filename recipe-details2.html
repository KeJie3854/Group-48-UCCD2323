Char Kuey Teow

Ingredients for Char Kuey Teow

  • 200g flat rice noodles (kuey teow)
  • 100g prawns, shelled and deveined
  • 50g bean sprouts
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili paste
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 stalks Chinese chives, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 2 tbsp water

Instructions for Char Kuey Teow

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Make sure all ingredients are prepped and ready to go as Char Kuey Teow is a quick stir-fry dish.
  2. Saut√© the Garlic and Prawns: Heat the cooking oil in a wok over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and stir-fry until fragrant. Add the prawns and cook until they turn pink.
  3. Add the Noodles: Push the prawns to the side of the wok and add the flat rice noodles. Stir-fry for a minute before adding the light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, and chili paste. Mix well to coat the noodles evenly.
  4. Add the Eggs: Push the noodles to one side of the wok and crack the eggs into the empty side. Scramble the eggs and then mix them into the noodles.
  5. Add Bean Sprouts and Chives: Add the bean sprouts, Chinese chives, and fish sauce to the wok. Stir-fry for another minute until everything is well combined and the vegetables are slightly wilted.
  6. Serve: Serve the Char Kuey Teow hot, garnished with extra chives or sliced red chilies if desired.
