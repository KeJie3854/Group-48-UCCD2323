Butterfly Blue Pea Nasi Lemak

Ingredients for Butterfly Blue Pea Nasi Lemak

  • 2 cups jasmine rice
  • 1 1/2 cups coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup butterfly pea flower tea (steeped and strained)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 pandan leaves, knotted
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 4 hard-boiled eggs, halved
  • 1/2 cup fried peanuts
  • 1/2 cup fried anchovies (ikan bilis)
  • Sambal (optional, for serving)

Instructions for Butterfly Blue Pea Nasi Lemak

  1. Prepare the Rice: Rinse the jasmine rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Drain and set aside.
  2. Cook the Rice: In a rice cooker or pot, combine the rinsed jasmine rice, coconut milk, butterfly pea flower tea, salt, sugar, pandan leaves, and cooking oil. Stir well to mix all the ingredients.
  3. Steam the Rice: If using a rice cooker, cook the rice according to the manufacturer's instructions. If using a pot, bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat, then reduce the heat to low and cover the pot. Allow the rice to steam for about 20 minutes, or until fully cooked and fluffy.
  4. Fluff and Serve: Once the rice is cooked, remove the pandan leaves and fluff the rice with a fork. Serve the Butterfly Blue Pea Nasi Lemak with sliced cucumber, hard-boiled eggs, fried peanuts, fried anchovies, and sambal on the side.
