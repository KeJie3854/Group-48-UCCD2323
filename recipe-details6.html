Apam Balik

Ingredients for Apam Balik

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup sweet corn kernels
  • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts, crushed
  • 1/4 cup sugar (for filling)

Instructions for Apam Balik

  1. Prepare the Batter: In a large bowl, mix together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Add the water, egg, and melted butter, whisking until smooth and well combined.
  2. Let the Batter Rest: Allow the batter to rest for about 30 minutes at room temperature. This will help in achieving a better texture for the apam balik.
  3. Cook the Apam Balik: Heat a non-stick skillet or frying pan over medium heat. Lightly grease the pan with a little oil or butter. Pour a ladleful of batter into the pan and spread it into a thin, even layer.
  4. Add Toppings: Sprinkle the sweet corn kernels and crushed peanuts over one half of the batter while it is still wet. Sprinkle with sugar as well.
  5. Fold and Cook: Once bubbles start to form on the surface and the edges look set, fold the apam balik in half to cover the fillings. Cook for another minute or until golden brown. Remove from the pan and set aside. Repeat with the remaining batter.
  6. Serve: Serve the apam balik warm. It can be enjoyed as a snack or dessert!
