Nasi Lemak Rendang Ayam

Ingredients for Nasi Lemak

  • 2 cups jasmine rice
  • 1 1/2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 pandan leaves, knotted
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil

Ingredients for Rendang Ayam

  • 500g chicken thighs, cut into pieces
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, minced
  • 2 red chilies, sliced
  • 2 tbsp rendang curry paste
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 1 tbsp palm sugar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 stalk lemongrass, bruised
  • 3 kaffir lime leaves, torn
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions for Nasi Lemak

  1. Prepare the Rice: Rinse the jasmine rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Drain and set aside.
  2. Cook the Rice: In a rice cooker or pot, combine the rinsed jasmine rice, coconut milk, water, salt, and pandan leaves. Stir well to mix all the ingredients.
  3. Steam the Rice: If using a rice cooker, cook the rice according to the manufacturer's instructions. If using a pot, bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat, then reduce the heat to low and cover the pot. Allow the rice to steam for about 20 minutes, or until fully cooked and fluffy.

Instructions for Rendang Ayam

  1. Sauté Aromatics: Heat cooking oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add chopped onions, garlic, and ginger. Sauté until fragrant and onions are soft.
  2. Add Chicken: Add chicken pieces and cook until they are browned on all sides.
  3. Cook the Rendang: Stir in the red chilies and rendang curry paste. Cook for a few minutes until the paste is well combined with the chicken. Add coconut milk, tamarind paste, palm sugar, soy sauce, lemongrass, and kaffir lime leaves. Stir well.
  4. Simmer: Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover and simmer for about 45 minutes, or until the chicken is tender and the sauce has thickened. Stir occasionally and add salt to taste.
  5. Serve: Serve the Rendang Ayam hot with the Nasi Lemak. Garnish with fresh coriander if desired.
